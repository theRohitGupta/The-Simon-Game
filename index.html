Wanna Play The Simon Game?

Press any KEY to START.
Simon will give the first signal.
Repeat the signal by pressing the same color button.
Everytime you do this right you'll get a LEVEL UP.
Then Simon will generate a new signal.
You need to repeat previous two signals by pressing the same color buttons,
In the similar order.
Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.
If you FAIL to repeat a sequence exactly.
Simon responds with a "RAZZ" sound.
This means you've lost, and the sequence of signals ends.
